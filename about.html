Who We Are

A Trusted National Brand in Specialty Alloys

Danyang Yixin Alloy Co., Ltd is located in the historic town of Lucheng in eastern Danyang City, within China's Yangtze River Delta Economic Zone. Registered in 2014, we are a national hi-tech enterprise specialising in vacuum induction melting, R&D, production and sales of superalloys, high-nickel alloys and nickel-base welding consumables.

Our products serve the petrochemical, aerospace, shipbuilding, energy, military, electronics, environmental, machinery and instrumentation sectors — backed by an ISO 9001:2015 quality management system and compliance with ASTM, AMS, AWS, BSI, GB/T, DIN, JIS, GOST and SS standards.
