Who We Are

A Trusted National Brand in Specialty Alloys

Danyang Yixin Alloy Co., Ltd is located in the historic town of Lucheng in eastern Danyang City, within China's Yangtze River Delta Economic Zone. Registered in 2014, we are a national hi-tech enterprise specialising in vacuum induction melting, R&D, production and sales of superalloys, high-nickel alloys and nickel-base welding consumables.

Our products serve the petrochemical, aerospace, shipbuilding, energy, military, electronics, environmental, machinery and instrumentation sectors — backed by an ISO 9001:2015 quality management system and compliance with ASTM, AMS, AWS, BSI, GB/T, DIN, JIS, GOST and SS standards.

Our Journey

Company History & Milestones

From a specialist start-up to a vertically integrated national hi-tech manufacturer of nickel alloys and welding consumables.

2014

Company Founded

Danyang Yixin Alloy Co., Ltd is registered in Lucheng, Danyang City, establishing its first vacuum induction melting workshop for specialty nickel alloys.

2016

R&D & Process Expansion

Added electroslag remelting and a full forging line, achieving end-to-end control from melt to finished product and broadening the grade portfolio.

2018

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Quality management system certified across all operations, with conformance to ASTM, AMS, AWS, GB/T, DIN and JIS standards for global supply.

2020

National Hi-Tech Enterprise

Recognised as a national high-tech enterprise, surpassing 30 invention and utility-model patents and partnering with 30+ research institutes and universities.

2022

Capacity Scaled to 2,000 T

Annual production scaled to 2,000 tonnes, serving petrochemical, aerospace, marine, energy and military sectors with consistent, certified material.

Today

Trusted by 1000+ Clients in 40+ Countries

A vertically integrated manufacturer supplying government and enterprise clients worldwide through dedicated export logistics and flexible OEM partnerships.
